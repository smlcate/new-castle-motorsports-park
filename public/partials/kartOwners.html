Own your own kart?

New Castle Motorsports Park is located an hour from Indianapolis, and is directly off of I-70.

NCMP's state of the art track hosts a variety of layouts, with the normal layout being 1.0 miles long. The track sees a
number of national races, and also has its own very competetive and fun local series.

Ready to join us? If you own a kart, you can come join us during any open practice or race weekend!
